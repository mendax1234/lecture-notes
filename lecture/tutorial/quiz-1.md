# Quiz 1

In Quiz 1, the trickiest question is definitely the last one. Two tips from prof is that:

1. The multiplier cannot be shared because sharing resource indicates at least one multiplexer at the input of the resources. And this will cause the critical path to be longer than required.
2. Similarly, if two functional resources are writing to the same register, a multiplexer will exist at the output of the two functional resources.
