## Course Spirit

In this course, we will use **thinking** and **doing** to strengthen our understanding of digital design.

> Studying for grade is useless. The more important thing is **what you know**.

In this course, the **top-down** approach is utilized, which means we will introduce **registers**, **combinational logic**, etc first and then lower down to transistor level stuff.
