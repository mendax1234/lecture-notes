### Weekly Schedule

#### Timing Recommendations

* ​**Start Week**<sub>**n+1**</sub>**&#x20;tasks on Week**<sub>**n**</sub>**&#x20;Friday**. As the Week<sub>n+1</sub> briefing is done on Week<sub>n</sub> Friday (i.e., \~2 days before the week actually starts), you are recommended to start on Week<sub>n</sub> tasks on that Friday itself. That way, you have more time to finish them by the Week<sub>n+1</sub> deadline (i.e., Week<sub>n+1</sub> Friday 1600).
* ​**Before attempting weekly project tasks, go through the weekly topics** (and do the weekly programming exercises/activities, if any) as the knowledge from those topics may be needed to complete the project tasks.
* ​**Try to finish most weekly tasks before attending the tutorial of that week**, because some tutorial tasks are done best _after_ weekly tasks have been completed, for the best learning experience.
